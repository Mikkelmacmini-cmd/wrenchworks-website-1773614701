One-page cheat-sheet

- What to save: How we work, not world facts
- How to write: One idea per entry; be concrete
- When to add: When a pattern emerges (3+ times) or a decision affects future work
- When to merge: Similar entries or clarifications; mark old as superseded
- Never delete: mark status: superseded and keep history
- Review cadence: Weekly quick pass; full rewrite monthly or when many changes
- Quick template fields: id, fact, category, timestamp, source, status, supersededBy

Remember: MEMORY.md helps people learn how you think. Keep it simple and useful.
