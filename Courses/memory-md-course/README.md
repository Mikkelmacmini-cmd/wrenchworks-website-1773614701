Memory.md - Beginner Course (6th-grade level)

What you'll find here:
- A simple explanation of what MEMORY.md is and why it matters
- How to keep tacit knowledge (thinking habits, preferences, lessons learned)
- Rules for when to add or merge facts
- Examples and short exercises you can do
- A template to create your own MEMORY.md entries
- A one-page cheat-sheet for quick reference

Goal: By the end you'll understand how to capture and care for tacit knowledge so it helps you and your team remember important ways you work.
