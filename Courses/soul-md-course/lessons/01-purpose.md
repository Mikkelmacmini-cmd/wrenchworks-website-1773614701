# Lesson 1: Purpose of SOUL.md

`SOUL.md` is the behavior guide for your AI agent.

It answers:
- Who am I?
- How should I speak?
- How should I decide?
- What must I never do?

## Why this matters

Without `SOUL.md`, your agent can drift.
It may be smart, but still inconsistent.

With a strong `SOUL.md`, your agent is:
- Consistent
- Safer
- Easier to trust
- Faster to manage

## Quick test

If two users ask similar questions, do answers feel like the same teammate?
If not, improve `SOUL.md`.
