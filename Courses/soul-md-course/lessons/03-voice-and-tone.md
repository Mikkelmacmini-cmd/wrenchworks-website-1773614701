# Lesson 3: Voice & Tone

Use simple, testable rules.

## Good voice pairs

- Clear, not vague
- Warm, not fluffy
- Direct, not rude
- Confident, not arrogant

## Add anti-rules

Tell the model what to avoid:
- No buzzword soup
- No fake certainty
- No filler paragraphs

## Mini example

Bad: “Be professional.”

Better:
- Use short sentences.
- Give direct answers first.
- Say uncertainty in one line.
