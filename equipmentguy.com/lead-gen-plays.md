# Lead Generation Plays — Implement Immediately

## 1) Offer-led CTA stack
- Primary CTA: "Book a 15-Min Equipment Planning Call"
- Secondary CTA: "Get a Facility Equipment Checklist"
- Tertiary CTA: "Request a Spec Review"

## 2) 5-minute audit funnel
Create a short self-assessment form:
- How many bays?
- Top bottleneck?
- Current lift age/brand?
- Expansion timeline?
Route to immediate consult scheduling.

## 3) Productized entry offers (no public pricing)
- Lift & Bay Throughput Assessment
- Collision Workflow Spec Review
- Heavy-Duty Expansion Plan
- Annual Inspection + Uptime Program

## 4) Local authority flywheel
- Weekly LinkedIn posts with real shop scenarios.
- Monthly "Equipment Mistakes" article.
- Before/after throughput case snapshots.

## 5) Conversion infrastructure
- CRM pipeline with stages: New Lead → Qualified → Site Review → Proposal → Won/Lost.
- SLA: response < 10 minutes during work hours.
- Auto follow-up cadence D1 / D3 / D7.

## 6) Emphasize digital gap as advantage (positioning)
Message: "Most equipment companies are offline-first and hard to evaluate online. EquipmentGuy brings clear, technical guidance into digital—so operators can make faster, safer decisions."
Use this in hero, about, and outreach.

## 7) Outreach scripts
DM opener:
"I help repair/collision facilities plan equipment upgrades that increase bay throughput and reduce downtime. Want a quick equipment plan for your current setup?"

Email subject ideas:
- quick equipment bottleneck idea for {{company}}
- reduce bay downtime with a better equipment plan
- 15-min equipment planning call?
