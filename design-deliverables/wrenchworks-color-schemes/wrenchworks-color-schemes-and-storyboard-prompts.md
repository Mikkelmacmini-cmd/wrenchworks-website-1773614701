# WrenchWorks — Full-Site Color Schemes + Storyboard Generation Prompts

## TL;DR
Three distinct visual directions are provided for WrenchWorks:
- **Brand A — Trustworthy Professional** (clean, dependable, service-first)
- **Brand B — Warm Premium** (craft quality, friendly sophistication)
- **Brand C — Bold Tech** (future-forward, high-performance)

For each brand:
1) Full hex palette (primary, secondary, accent, neutral, background)
2) Usage rules (headlines, CTA, backgrounds)
3) Sample animation jacket color
4) Seven highly detailed storyboard prompts with composition, lighting, focal point, props, mood, negative prompts, and responsive crop suggestions.

# Brand A — Trustworthy Professional

## A1) Color Palette (Hex)
- **Primary:** `#0F3D63` (Deep Trust Blue)
- **Secondary:** `#2E6F95` (Clear Steel Blue)
- **Accent:** `#F4A62A` (Service Signal Amber)
- **Neutral:** `#6B7280` (Balanced Slate Gray)
- **Background:** `#F5F8FC` (Cool Clean Off-White)

## A2) Usage Rules
- **Headlines:** Use `#0F3D63` on light backgrounds; reserve all-caps only for short labels.
- **CTA Buttons:** Primary CTA fill `#F4A62A` with dark text `#0F2236`; hover darken to `#DB921E`.
- **Secondary CTA / Links:** `#2E6F95` text or outline buttons with `#2E6F95` border.
- **Section Backgrounds:** Alternating `#F5F8FC` and white to create service-card rhythm.
- **Data/Trust Elements:** Use `#0F3D63` + `#2E6F95`; avoid overusing accent in body copy.

## A3) Animation Main Character Jacket Color
- **Recommended jacket:** `#2E6F95` with subtle piping in `#0F3D63` and optional badge accents in `#F4A62A`.

## A4) Storyboard Prompts (7 Scenes)

### Scene 1 — Arrival / Trust Establishment
**Prompt:**
Create a polished cinematic frame for an animated auto-service brand. Wide 24mm lens perspective from slightly low eye level, showing a branded WrenchWorks service van arriving in a quiet suburban driveway at early morning. Composition uses rule of thirds: van occupies left third, homeowner and front porch on right third, open sky and tree line across upper third. Main mechanic steps out confidently wearing a steel-blue jacket (#2E6F95), navy undershirt (#0F3D63), and amber tool tag accent (#F4A62A). Lighting is soft directional sunrise with cool fill shadows, realistic reflective highlights on van panels, and a clean, trustworthy color grade leaning blue-neutral. Focal point is the mechanic’s open-hand greeting gesture and visible logo badge. Include props: organized tool case, tablet for work order, clean driveway cone, subtle wrench icon on van door, and neatly parked customer vehicle in frame depth. Mood should feel dependable, calm, punctual, and professional.
**Negative prompts:** chaotic clutter, greasy overalls, aggressive contrast, neon oversaturation, damaged tools, angry expressions, dramatic storm weather, dystopian vibe, exaggerated caricature proportions, unreadable logos.
**Responsive crop suggestions:**
1) **16:9 desktop:** Keep van nose + mechanic + homeowner handshake triangle centered-left.
2) **4:5 mobile feed:** Tighten to mechanic and homeowner with van logo still visible behind shoulder.
3) **9:16 story:** Vertical emphasis on mechanic stepping from van, logo at chest level, porch depth behind.

### Scene 2 — Diagnostic Inspection
**Prompt:**
Generate a medium-wide service bay/driveway diagnostic shot with a 35mm lens look, camera at chest height angled 20 degrees toward vehicle front-left. Mechanic kneels by wheel assembly while holding a diagnostic scanner linked to a tablet UI. Palette should reinforce professionalism: deep navy interfaces (#0F3D63), clear blue apparel (#2E6F95), amber status indicators (#F4A62A), cool off-white background surfaces (#F5F8FC). Lighting is balanced daylight plus subtle bounce card fill to preserve detail in shadows under the vehicle. Focal point is the scanner screen glow reflecting on mechanic glove and the mechanic’s focused expression. Include props: torque wrench on clean mat, labeled parts tray, safety gloves, wheel chocks, tablet with checklist ticks, and a discreet customer observing from background blur. Mood should be methodical, transparent, and competent.
**Negative prompts:** messy workshop chaos, sparks flying, unnecessary smoke, fantasy holograms, dark horror shadows, random brand logos, oil splatter over everything, blurry faces, fisheye distortion.
**Responsive crop suggestions:**
1) **16:9 desktop:** Vehicle front quarter on left, mechanic centered, tablet on right foreground.
2) **1:1 social tile:** Center scanner + mechanic hands + wheel detail for technical credibility.
3) **9:16 mobile story:** Vertical stack with mechanic face top third, scanner center, wheel/brake bottom.
