## 1) Final Messaging Position (Brand Sentence)

**AutoTrek is the diagnosis-first shop in Littleton that gives drivers a clear repair plan, explicit approval control, and warranty-backed confidence before money is committed.**

This is the center of gravity for all homepage and service-page language.

---

## 2) Voice Lock (What “Premium” Sounds Like Here)

Use these standards in every module:
- **Specific over abstract:** “4.9★ from 600+ local reviews” beats “trusted by the community.”
- **Operational over promotional:** “No repairs start until you approve” beats “honest service.”
- **Decision-led over informational:** “Can I keep driving?” beats “learn more about symptoms.”
- **Calm authority over hype:** no chest-thumping, no fluffy superlatives.

### Red-flag words to remove from high-intent sections
`quality`, `comprehensive`, `state-of-the-art`, `learn more`, `contact us`, `best service`

Each high-intent service page must include this sequence and copy style:

1. **Common Symptoms**
