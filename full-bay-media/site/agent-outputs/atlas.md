# AutoTrek SEO Strategy + Keyword Map + Title/Meta Recommendations

## 1) Executive SEO Strategy (Local + Service-Led)

### Primary goal
Increase qualified calls and appointment requests from **Littleton + nearby city** searches by pairing:
1. A strong homepage (brand + trust + local intent),
2. A services hub (topic authority + internal linking),
3. Individual service pages (transactional intent),
4. City pages (geo-modified demand capture).

### Strategic model
- **Homepage** targets broad commercial intent: `auto repair littleton co`, `auto service center littleton`.
- **Services hub** targets category intent: `auto repair services littleton`.
- **Service pages** target high-conversion service+city terms: `brake repair littleton co`, etc.
- **City pages** target geo-entry terms for nearby markets and funnel to top services.

### SEO priorities by impact
1. **Fix metadata targeting and uniqueness** (fast win, immediate indexing clarity).
2. **Build city pages with unique local proof + internal service blocks** (largest traffic upside).
3. **Add Service + FAQ schema on service pages** (CTR/rich result support).
4. **Strengthen internal links** between homepage ↔ services hub ↔ services ↔ city pages.
5. **Add conversion/supportive content blocks** (cost, symptoms, timeline, FAQs).

## 4) Internal Linking Blueprint

- Homepage hero + service cards → top 6 money service pages.
- Services hub → all individual service pages + all city pages.
- Each service page:
  - Link to 2 related services (e.g., brakes ↔ alignment ↔ suspension).
  - Link to all city pages via “Areas We Serve” module.
- Each city page:
  - Link to 4 highest-converting services (brakes, diagnostics, maintenance, alignment).
  - Link back to /services and homepage.

Anchor text examples:
- “brake repair in Littleton”
- “check engine diagnostics”
- “fleet maintenance services”
- “auto repair near Highlands Ranch”

## 5) Content Blocks to Add for Ranking + Conversion

Add to each service page:
- **Symptoms we solve** (real search language)
- **What’s included** (scope clarity)
- **Price/timing expectations** (trust + conversion)
- **FAQ (3–5 service-specific Qs)**
- **ServiceArea mentions** woven naturally
- **Strong CTA at top/mid/bottom**

Add to each city page:
- Unique intro paragraph about that city + driving context
- “Popular services for [City] drivers” section
- 2 local trust proofs (reviews, years in business, warranty)
- FAQ tailored to distance/scheduling/turnaround
- Driving directions + map CTA

## 6) Technical SEO Recommendations

1. Add explicit page metadata for homepage in `src/app/layout.tsx` or dedicated home metadata export.
2. Ensure canonical URLs are absolute and consistent across all pages.
3. Add JSON-LD:
   - Homepage: `AutoRepair` (already present, keep).
   - Service pages: `Service` + `FAQPage`.
   - City pages: `LocalBusiness` mention + areaServed context.
4. Create `locations` sitemap entries once city pages launch.
5. Compress/serve WebP where possible for hero and service images.
