---

## 1) Voice Shift (What Changes Now)

Move from generic “quality service” language to **evidence-led authority**:

- **From:** “We provide reliable repairs.”
- **To:** “You get a clear diagnosis, line-item estimate, and no repairs start until you approve.”

- **From:** “Trusted by customers.”
- **To:** “4.9★ Google rating, serving Littleton since 2009, backed by a 3-year/36,000-mile warranty.”

- **From:** “Schedule service today.”
- **To:** “Call now, request a callback, or get a 60-second quick diagnosis.”

Tone target: confident, plainspoken, specific.

---

## 2) CTA System (Clean + Intent-Based)

## Primary CTA labels (use by context)
- **Call Now** (urgent/safety concern)
- **Get Quick Diagnosis** (uncertain symptom)
- **Request Callback** (after-hours or at-work users)

## CTA helper lines
- **Call Now:** “Speak with a service advisor in minutes.”
- **Get Quick Diagnosis:** “Answer 2–3 questions and get your best next step.”
- **Request Callback:** “We’ll call you back with guidance and availability.”

## Mobile sticky rail copy
- `Call Now`
- `Request Callback`
- `Quick Diagnosis`

Do not use vague CTA text like “Learn More,” “Get Started,” or “Contact Us” in high-intent modules.
