## Implementation Notes (for design/dev)
- **White direction:** keep shorter lines, lower emotional intensity, and precision language.
- **Green direction:** favor reassurance words (trust, confidence, long-term, local, care).
- **Red direction:** front-load urgency words (today, now, fast, immediate).
- Reuse proof chips by direction in hero + sticky CTA region for visual/copy cohesion.
- Keep one dominant primary CTA label per page state to avoid split intent.
