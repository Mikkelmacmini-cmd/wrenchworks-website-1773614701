# Full Bay Media — Brand Starter Kit

## Brand Positioning
**Full Bay Media** helps independent auto repair shops get found online, earn trust fast, and book more profitable jobs.

## One-Liner
We build clean, high-converting websites and local growth systems for independent auto repair shops.

## Tagline Options
1. Fill Every Bay.
2. Built for Independent Shops.
3. More Cars. Better Jobs. Full Bays.
4. Local Growth for Real Repair Shops.

## Elevator Pitch (30 sec)
Full Bay Media is a growth partner for independent repair shops. We combine premium websites, local SEO, Google Business Profile optimization, and automated review funnels so shops rank higher, look more trustworthy, and keep their bays full.

## Core Services (Day One)
- Website Design & Build (conversion-focused)
- Local SEO Optimization
- Google Business Profile Optimization & Management
- Automated Review Funnel Setup

## Brand Personality
- Practical
- Trustworthy
- Blue-collar respectful
- Sharp and modern
- Results-driven, never hypey

## Voice & Tone
- Plainspoken, direct, confident
- No fluff, no jargon overload
- Respectful of shop owners’ time
- Focus on outcomes: calls, bookings, and trust

## Visual Direction
Style: Clean, minimal, premium, approachable.

### Color Palette
- **Oil Black**: `#111315` (primary text/background accents)
- **Steel Gray**: `#39424E` (secondary)
- **Shop White**: `#F7F8FA` (background)
- **Signal Blue**: `#2B6EF3` (CTAs, links)
- **Safety Amber**: `#F5A524` (small highlights)

### Typography
- Headings: **Sora** (or **Montserrat** fallback)
- Body: **Inter**
- Buttons/UI: **Inter SemiBold**

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Built for independents, not dealerships**
2. **Trust-first local visibility**
3. **Simple systems that actually run**
4. **Measurable growth, month over month**

## Quick Brand Rules
- Prefer “shop” over “business” in copy
- Use short paragraphs and bullet points
- Show before/after outcomes whenever possible
- Every page should end in one clear CTA
