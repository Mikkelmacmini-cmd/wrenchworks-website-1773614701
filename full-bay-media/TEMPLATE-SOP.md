# Full Bay Media — Auto Repair Website Production SOP

> Scope: **Auto Repair shops only** (no collision scope in this SOP).

## 1) Product Tiers

### Tier 1 — Starter Local Presence (3–5 days)
- Home
- Services Hub
- 5 Core Service Pages
- About
- Reviews
- Contact / Book Call
- Service Area (single page)

### Tier 2 — Growth Engine (7–14 days)
Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- 15–30 SEO pages total (service + city + specialty)
- Financing page
- Specials/Coupons page
- Case Studies page
- Careers page (optional)

### Tier 3 — Market Dominator (14–30 days)
Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Multi-location architecture (hub + location pages)
- 50+ programmatic SEO pages
- Resource center/blog hub
- Video/testimonial library
- Paid campaign landing pages

---

## 2) Build Sequence (Always Follow)

1. Discovery + Intake
2. Offer/CTA alignment
3. Sitemap + page count lock
4. Block library assembly
5. Homepage build
6. Service template build
7. SEO page rollout
8. Proof/reviews/case studies
9. Conversion wiring
10. QA + launch

---

## 3) Intake Checklist (Required Before Build)

- Business name, phone, email
- Booking URL (or call-first decision)
- Primary location + service area list
- Service list (top 5 required)
- Team photos + shop photos
- Logo assets (SVG/PNG)
- Existing reviews/testimonials
- Warranty/financing/specials details
- Compliance notes (claims, legal language)

---

## 4) CTA System (Standard)

- Primary CTA sitewide: **Book a Call**
- Secondary CTA: **Call Now**
- No dead anchors (`#`) for primary CTAs.
- Every major section should include a conversion path.

---

## 5) Required Page Blocks

## Home
- Hero (clear niche/value statement)
- Trust chips (rating, years, warranty)
- Services tiles
- Process / roadmap section
- Proof block (case snippets or logos)
- FAQ (click accordion)
- Final CTA

## Service Page Template
- Hero + CTA
- Who it’s for
- Symptoms/signs
- What’s included
- **Expected outcomes (30/60/90 days)**
- FAQ
- CTA

## Contact
- Click-to-call
- Booking link
- Lead form
- Hours + map + address

## Case Study
- Before state
- Work completed
- After metrics
- Screenshots
- CTA

---

## 6) SEO Rollout Standard

Tier 1:
- Core technical setup
- Service pages for top revenue services

Tier 2:
- Service x city pages
- Internal linking map
- Structured headings and metadata

Tier 3:
- Programmatic service/location expansions
- Ongoing content cadence
- Monthly optimization sprints

---

## 7) Performance Standard

- Convert large active assets to WebP/AVIF where safe
- Preload hero and logo assets
- Avoid layout shift (set image dimensions)
- Compress and lazy-load non-critical media

---

## 8) Mobile UX Standard

- Sticky mobile CTA
- Click-safe FAQ accordion (no hover-only logic)
- Tap targets >= 44px
- Keep hero copy short and readable

---

## 9) Integrations & Tracking

- GA4 installed
- Conversion events:
  - phone click
  - form submit
  - booking click
- Search Console connected
- GBP link consistency check

---

## 10) QA Checklist (Pre-Launch)

- All CTAs wired
- No broken links/404s
- No placeholder text/images
- Mobile and desktop visual pass complete
- Forms tested end-to-end
- Page speed baseline acceptable
- Schema + metadata present

---

## 11) Handoff Package

- Live URLs + sitemap
- Credentials handoff
- 1-page “How to update site” guide
- 30-day optimization recommendations

---

## 12) Out of Scope (for this SOP)

- Collision-only website builds
- Ecommerce flows
- Complex custom web app features

(Collision package will be handled in a separate SOP.)
