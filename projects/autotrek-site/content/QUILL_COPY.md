### H2: Signs You May Need Brake Service
If you notice any of these warning signs, schedule a brake inspection as soon as possible:
- Squeaking, squealing, or grinding noises
- Vibrations while braking
- Soft or spongy brake pedal
- Increased stopping distance
- Brake warning light on
- Vehicle pulling when braking

Prompt brake service can help prevent more expensive repairs and keep your car safe to drive.

**How often should I have my brakes checked?**  
At least once a year, or sooner if you notice noise, vibration, or reduced braking performance.
