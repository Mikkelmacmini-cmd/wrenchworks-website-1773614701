## 4) On-Page SEO Requirements

## Global Requirements (All Money Pages)
1. **Title tag**: 50–60 chars, starts with primary keyword + city + brand.
2. **Meta description**: 140–160 chars with trust element + CTA + city.
3. **Single H1** containing service + city modifier.
4. **Opening 100 words** include primary keyword naturally.
5. **Trust block above fold**: 36/36 warranty, NAPA Service Center, ASE/experience proof.
6. **NAP consistency** in footer and contact page:
   - AutoTrek Service Center
   - 8161 S Grant Way, Littleton, CO 80122
   - (303) 328-3356
7. **Clear conversion CTA** repeated 3+ times/page: “Schedule an Appointment” + click-to-call.
8. **FAQ section** (2–5 Qs) for long-tail capture and schema eligibility.
9. **Internal links** to 3–6 related services + 1 city page + contact page.
10. **Image SEO**: compressed webp + descriptive alt text (`brake-repair-littleton-co`).

## Internal Linking Rules
- Services hub links to every service money page.
- Every service page links back to Services hub + Contact + at least two sibling services.
- City pages link to top 5 high-demand services.
- Home page features direct links to top 6 services.

## Schema Recommendations
- **LocalBusiness / AutoRepair** schema on home + contact + location pages.
- **Service** schema on each service page.
- **FAQPage** schema where visible FAQs exist.
- **Review/AggregateRating** only if compliant with Google guidelines and sourced transparently.
- **BreadcrumbList** schema sitewide.

## Technical SEO Minimums
- Fast Core Web Vitals (LCP <2.5s, CLS <0.1, INP <200ms target)
- XML sitemap + robots.txt + canonical tags
- Noindex thin/duplicate utility pages
- Proper GA4 + call tracking + form conversion events when IDs are available

## 5) Competitor Gap Notes (Littleton Market)

Competitors reviewed: Pro Auto Care, Randy’s Auto Care, Hartman Automotive, Hinsdale Automotive (+ Rocky Mountain Eurosport not fetchable due Cloudflare block).

### Observed Competitor Strengths
- **Pro Auto Care:** strong multi-location architecture, city/service landing pages, financing + warranty messaging.
- **Hinsdale Automotive:** deep service catalog + broad service area pages (large keyword surface area).
- **Hartman Automotive:** clean trust-forward UX and social proof presentation.
- **Randy’s Auto Care:** established longevity messaging and transparent service history angle.

### Common Weaknesses / Gaps AutoTrek Can Exploit
1. **Overloaded navigation and noisy page structure** on several competitor sites; opportunity for cleaner IA and better UX.
2. **Thin differentiation copy** (many generic “we care” statements). AutoTrek can win with process-driven, transparent service content.
3. **Inconsistent service depth:** many mention services without robust educational/supporting content.
4. **Limited EV/hybrid authority storytelling** despite listing service.
5. **Inconsistent conversion hierarchy** (CTA placement and urgency not always clear).

### Positioning Opportunities for AutoTrek
- Lead with a **premium-but-clear** service promise: “dealer-level capability without dealership friction.”
- Make **EV & Hybrid Repair** a signature pillar (dedicated proof, diagnostics process, safety standards).
- Build stronger **service + city intent pages** with unique local copy, FAQs, and clearer next steps.
- Use trust anchors repeatedly: **NAPA + 36/36 warranty + review proof + diagnostic transparency**.

### Quick-Win Roadmap (First 60 Days)
1. Launch core money pages (top 8 services + Littleton page).
2. Publish 4 secondary city pages with localized intros + driving-distance cues.
3. Add FAQ schema on top pages.
4. Build internal link blocks by symptom and by service type.
5. Start 2 posts/month on high-intent maintenance/diagnostic topics.
