Exercises — Practice writing SOUL.md

Lesson 1
- Write one sentence that says why your project exists.

Lesson 2
- Write one short rule your project needs.

Lesson 3
- Write the mission (10–20 words) and one example sentence in your chosen voice.

Lesson 4
- Write two rules and one boundary.

Lesson 5
- Write one example command and add three helpful links.

Lesson 6
- Run the review checklist and make two edits.

How to submit
- Save your draft as SOUL.md and open a PR, or share it with your team chat.
