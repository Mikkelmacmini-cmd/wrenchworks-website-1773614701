Lesson 6 — Review and publish

Goal: Check your SOUL.md and share it.

Review checklist
- Is the mission one clear sentence?
- Are rules short and useful?
- Are contacts listed?
- Is voice consistent?
- Is the file one page?

Publish
- Put SOUL.md at the repo root.
- Link to it from README.
- Tell the team and ask for quick feedback.

Exercise
- Run the checklist on your draft and make two small edits.
