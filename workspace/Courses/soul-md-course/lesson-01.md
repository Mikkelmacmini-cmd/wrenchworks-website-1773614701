Lesson 1 — What is SOUL.md?

Goal: Learn what SOUL.md is and why it matters.

What it is
- SOUL.md is a short file that explains the heart of a project.
- It tells people why the project exists and how the team works.

Why it matters
- Helps new people join fast.
- Keeps the team focused.
- Makes decisions easier.

Quick tip
- Keep it short. One page is great.

Exercise
- Write one sentence that says why your project exists.
