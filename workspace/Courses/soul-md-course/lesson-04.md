Lesson 4 — Rules and boundaries

Goal: Make clear rules and what you will not do.

Rules
- Keep rules short (one line each).
- Make rules useful and actionable.
- Example: "Run tests before merging." "Document changes that affect users."

Boundaries
- Say what the project will not do.
- Example: "We won't support old browsers." "We don't take feature requests in issues."

Exercise
- Write two rules and one boundary for your project.
