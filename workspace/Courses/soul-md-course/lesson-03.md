Lesson 3 — Writing the mission and voice

Goal: Write a clear mission sentence and pick a voice.

Mission tips
- Use simple words.
- Say who it helps and what it does.
- Keep it 10–20 words.

Voice tips
- Choose 2 words: e.g., "friendly" and "direct".
- Give one example sentence in that voice.

Example
- Mission: "Help small teams ship useful features fast."
- Voice: Friendly, direct. Example sentence: "Tell us what you need in one line."

Exercise
- Write a mission for your project (10–20 words).
- Write one example sentence in your chosen voice.
