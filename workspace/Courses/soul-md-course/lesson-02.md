Lesson 2 — The important sections

Goal: Learn the common parts of a SOUL.md file.

Common sections
- Mission: One sentence about the goal.
- Voice & Tone: How the project speaks.
- Boundaries: What the project will not do.
- Rules: Short, practical rules for the team.
- Contacts: Who to ask for help.

Why these matter
- Mission: keeps choices aligned.
- Voice: keeps writing consistent.
- Boundaries: stop scope creep.
- Rules: make work smoother.

Exercise
- Pick one rule your project needs (e.g., "Ask before changing the homepage"). Write it down.
