SOUL.md Cheat Sheet — Quick help (one page)

Mission
- One sentence: who + what + value. Keep it short.
- Example: "Help small teams ship features that solve real user problems."

Voice
- Pick two adjectives (e.g., friendly, direct).
- Write one example sentence in that voice.

Rules
- 2–5 short rules. Make them actionable.
- Example: "Run tests before merging."

Boundaries
- List 1–3 things you will not do.
- Helps stop scope creep.

Structure (order)
- Mission
- Voice & Tone
- Rules
- Boundaries
- Contacts
- Links

Publish
- Put SOUL.md at repo root and link from README.
- Ask team for a quick read and sign-off.

Keep it short. Keep it useful.
