# PipelinePilot — 24h Launch Business (Autonomous-Operator Model)

## Mission
Launch a high-margin B2B service in 24 hours targeting $10k+/month net profit.

## Business Model
PipelinePilot installs and operates a founder's outbound appointment pipeline.

### Core Offer
"12 Appointments in 30 Days" System:
- ICP + offer angle
- 500-lead build
- Cold email + LinkedIn sequence stack
- Inbox handling playbooks
- Weekly optimization report

### Pricing
- Launch Plan: $1,500 setup + $1,000/mo
- Growth Plan: $2,500 setup + $1,500/mo
- Performance Add-on: $500 bonus per qualified meeting above baseline

## Profit Target Math
Example @ 10 active Growth clients:
- Revenue: 10 x $1,500 = $15,000 MRR
- Delivery tools/ops cost estimate: ~$3,500
- Net profit: ~$11,500/month

## 24h Launch Stack
- Website: one-page conversion site
- Payments: Stripe links (setup fee + recurring)
- Intake: Tally/Typeform form
- CRM: Notion or Airtable board
- Support: macro-based support desk workflow
