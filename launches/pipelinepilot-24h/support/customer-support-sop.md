# Customer Support SOP

## Response SLA
- New lead/support inquiry: < 30 min during active hours
- Client requests: same business day

## Macros
### New Inquiry
"Thanks for reaching out — if you share your offer + target customer, I can map your pipeline fit today."

### Payment Confirmation
"You’re in. Next step: complete intake so we can start build immediately."

### Escalation
"I’m escalating this to delivery ops now. You’ll get an update in the next 2 hours."
