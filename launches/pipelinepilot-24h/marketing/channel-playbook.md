# Channel Playbook

## Primary channels
1) Direct outreach (LinkedIn + email)
2) Founder communities
3) Short authority posts with CTA

## 3 launch post hooks
- "Most founders don't need more leads. They need a pipeline that runs daily."
- "I opened 10 spots to build outbound systems in 7 days for founders."
- "If your calendar is inconsistent, your pipeline is broken."

## DM opener
"Quick one: we build and run appointment pipelines for founders. If useful, I can send a 3-step plan for your offer."
